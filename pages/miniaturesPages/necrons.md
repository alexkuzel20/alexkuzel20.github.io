# Necron Miniatures (2020)

[back](https://alexkuzel20.github.io/pages/miniature_archive)

Necrons are another happy addition to the "My Brother Painted These Models One Color And I Stole Them And Added More Paint" family <3

Not too much to say here, my goal with most of these was to get them to the table, and at the end of the day I feel like these necron models aren't detailed enough to really flex my painting abilities. Some of these are good, some are just me going through the motions.

*8. Oh yeah. Each limb of this model is chopped up piecemeal to get this pose, and I had been getting practice on Power Sword gradients from a Deathwatch commission I had done for Aaron. The shield is made from the top of a Tau drone, so the idea here is essentially that this Necron murdered a T'au soldier and used their drone as a tool/trophy.*

---

![necrons9](/assets/img/miniatures/necrons/09.jpg)
*9. Previous model and the rest are Flayed Ones. Lore-wise, I'm pretty sure they're Necrons who believe they're still mortal and cloak themselves in flesh in their delusion; but finance-wise, the official sculpts cost like 60$ for 5 models so I took regular Necron Warriors, glued swords on their hands and put some greenstuff on them which I painted tan. Fun fact, the blades on this models arms are actually LEGO cutlasses!
