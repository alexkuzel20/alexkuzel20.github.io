*31. One of the big things that pushed me away from painting D&D miniatures is that I would spend hours and hours painting monsters that would never touch the tabletop. At least with wargaming, your entire squad/army gets to be on the table each game, but aside from player characters, I rarely got to use most of these models, and would often represent a person or creature in-game with a scrap of paper if I didn't have exactly the right model.*
