*The Reaper Bones line, where most of my D&D miniatures are from, are pre-primed, single piece miniatures that might not be of the highest level of detail, but you could throw them at a wall and no pieces would break off. I remember soaking bits of the rubbery plastic in hot water to attempt to straighten out warped pieces (such as this knight's sword) to little success.*
